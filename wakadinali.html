wakadinali members

scar mkadinali

Churchil Mandela better known as Scar Mkadinali was born and raised in Nairobi Eastlands Areas of Umoja 1 When you talk about all the struggles and the harsh reality of life that people living in the slums face every day, Scar Mkadinali has seen it all since he was a little kid

Domani munga

Domani Munga, real name David Munga Ramadhan, is the second member of the group Wakadinali. His nicknames are Wuzu, Dochman Mwizi, and Doche. Domani is a lyricist and has mastered the game ever since he came out. Fans applaud him for his catchy hooks and choruses, which are relatable in modern society. Indeed, Wakadinali is not Wakadinali without Domani Munga.

sewersydaa

Sewersydaa, also known as Mr. Umbwa, Chief Gwetheist, or Uncle Trench (real name Salim Ali Tangut), started off as an editor and DOP before joining Wakadinali as an artist. He is best known for his edits under the name Slim Visuals. He directed and edited several Wakadinali music videos before Wakadinali even became famous. One of his most notable projects is Wakadinali’s “Morio Anzenza” music video, featuring Dyana Cods
